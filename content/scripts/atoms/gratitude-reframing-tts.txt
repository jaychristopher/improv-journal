[emphatic] Before. That's the whole thing. Gratitude after judgment is too late. [teaching] You build on the offer immediately. Before your evaluating mind catches up. Your partner says you're a time-traveling dentist and instead of going "what?" you go "oh, of course" and the scene has somewhere to go.
