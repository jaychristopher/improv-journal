```
How to stop overthinking — it's not a willpower problem, it's a bandwidth problem.

Improv performers walk on stage every night with no script and no plan. They've discovered that overthinking is caused by "internal computation" — the planning loop that steals the attention you need for the actual moment. The fix isn't trying harder. It's redirecting where your attention goes.

In this video:
- The bandwidth model — why willpower can't fix overthinking
- What improv performers discover about getting out of your head
- Three exercises you can try today: Mirroring, First Line Drill, Last Word Response

Full written breakdown with more exercises:
```
