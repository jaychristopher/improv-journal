## Competitor Intelligence

### improvencyclopedia.org (~3,200 traffic/mo)
- #1 for "improv games" (3,200 vol) — simple game directory, no depth
- #3 for "the harold" (400 vol)
- #7 for "story spine" (400 vol)
- #2 for "big booty game" (250 vol)
- **Weakness:** No pedagogy, no paths, no audio, no theory — just game listings

### learnimprov.com (~150 traffic/mo)
- #3 for "improv warm up games" (200 vol)
- #1 for "freeze improv game" (70 vol)
- #2 for "long form improv" (50 vol)
- **Weakness:** Very basic content, no structured learning

### Our competitive advantages
- Structured learning paths with progression
- Multi-tradition theory content (unique)
- Audio on all threads
- Knowledge graph architecture with atom-level linking
- Practice recommendations connected to theory
- Life-application bridges (non-performer audience)
