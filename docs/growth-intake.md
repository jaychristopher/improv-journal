## About the product

1. **What is your product?** The Physics of Connection (physicsofconnection.com) — A knowledge graph for the art of human connection, grounded in 60 years of improv performance science.
2. **Who is your ideal customer?** Five personas: (A) New-to-improv beginners, (B) Life-application seekers solving personal problems (overthinking, awkwardness, confidence), (C) Team leaders/facilitators wanting team exercises, (D) Stuck improvisers wanting to get better, (E) Performers pushing toward mastery, (F) Teachers.
3. **What problem does it solve?** Applies improv principles to everyday human challenges — with depth, exercises, and multi-tradition sourcing that generic self-help sites can't match.
4. **Pricing model:** Free. No paywall. Revenue model TBD (future: courses, workshops, consulting).
5. **Paying customers:** 0 (free site).
6. **Current MRR:** $0.

## What's new from the expanded playbook (not in our current growth plan)

### Strategies to ADD:

1. **Subreddit discovery workflow** — Use ads.reddit.com targeting tool + Map of Reddit (anvaka.github.io/map-of-reddit) to find hidden communities. More structured than our current list.

2. **"Do unsolicited work" strategy** — When someone posts about team dysfunction, communication problems, or personal growth struggles on Reddit/LinkedIn, create a mini-analysis using our frameworks and drop it in the comments. Shows our expertise in action.

3. **Build in public** — Share the journey of building a knowledge graph for improv. This is genuinely interesting: a Zettelkasten for human connection, multi-tradition analysis, 155 atoms linked together. The architecture itself is the story.

4. **Complementary plug technique** — When posting about our content, always mention one other resource alongside ours. Makes it look like a genuine recommendation, not self-promotion.

5. **Reddit posting templates** — Five post types (Relatable, Guide, Conversation Starter, Story, Case Study) adapted for our content. More structured than "be helpful."

6. **Keyword-optimized Reddit posting** — Write Reddit post titles using actual search keywords so the thread ranks on Google AND AI search.

7. **Free tools for adjacent communities** — Beyond exercise picker: scene failure diagnostic, conversation skill assessment, team meeting warm-up generator.
